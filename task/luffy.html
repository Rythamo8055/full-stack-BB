Devil Fruit

Japanese Name: Gomu Gomu no Mi (Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika)

English Name: Gum-Gum Fruit (Human-Human Fruit, Model: Nika)

Meaning: Rubber; Human; Nika

Type: Paramecia (Mythical Zoan)

Luffy's primary ability comes from the Gomu Gomu no Mi, which turned his body into rubber, allowing him to stretch and inflate his limbs. After awakening, his Devil Fruit was revealed to be the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika, a Mythical Zoan type, granting him the properties of the Sun God Nika, allowing him to fight with boundless imagination and bring smiles to people's faces. 0

Physical Strength & Combat

Luffy possesses exceptional physical strength, enhanced by his Devil Fruit. He has undergone intensive combat training and developed various "Gear" forms (Gear 2, Gear 3, Gear 4) to increase his speed, power, and defense. 0

Personality

Luffy is known for his reckless behavior, but his crew trusts him implicitly. He has an unwavering faith in his crew and treats them equally, which solidifies their loyalty. He sees the Pirate King role as one of mutual aid, not dominance, and inspires other pirates with his daring actions. 0
