Personality

Jinbe is a highly honorable and compassionate individual, deeply committed to justice and the well-being of others, especially his fellow fish-men. He is calm, wise, and often acts as a voice of reason within the Straw Hat Pirates. He is known for his unwavering loyalty and his willingness to sacrifice himself for his friends. 0
