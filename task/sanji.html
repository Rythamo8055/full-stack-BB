Powers and Abilities

Black Leg Style

Sanji is a master of the Black Leg Style, a martial art that focuses on powerful kicks. He refrains from using his hands in combat to protect them for cooking. His kicks are strong enough to shatter rocks and defeat formidable opponents. 0

Haki

Sanji possesses both Busoshoku (Armament) and Kenbunshoku (Observation) Haki, which he uses to enhance his attacks and sense his opponents' movements. 0

Personality

Sanji is a chivalrous and passionate individual, deeply devoted to women and cooking. He often flirts with women and has a strong sense of justice, especially when it comes to protecting the weak. Despite his suave demeanor, he can be hot-headed and gets into frequent arguments with Zoro. 0
